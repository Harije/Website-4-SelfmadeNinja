Our Story

At Selfmade Ninja Academy, our mission is to revolutionize the way people learn and experience technology. Our story began with a burning desire to make a difference in the education industry. We recognized that the traditional methods of teaching were outdated, and that technology could be harnessed to create a more dynamic and immersive learning experience. This realization led us to create a unique tool that allows learners to connect and collaborate in a way that was never possible before. The Covid-19 pandemic provided an opportunity for us to put our tool to the test, and we were thrilled to see the positive impact it had on the learning experience of thousands of students. At Selfmade Ninja Academy, we believe that learning and experience go hand in hand, and our approach has been validated by the incredible growth we have experienced.
